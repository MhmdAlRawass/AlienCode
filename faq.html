Q1. What services does AlienCode provide?

AlienCode offers a comprehensive range of IT services, including web and mobile application development, UI/UX and branding identity design, IT management and consulting, digital marketing, and data security solutions. We deliver tailor-made digital products for businesses of any size and sector.

Q2. Why should I choose AlienCode over other IT companies?

AlienCode combines 25 years of hands-on experience with a commitment to modern technology and proven best practices. Our approach blends traditional business values with innovative digital solutions, ensuring every project is built to last, fully secure, and ready to adapt to your future needs.

Q3. What is the typical process for starting a project with AlienCode?

We start with a free consultation to understand your business needs. Then, we provide a detailed project proposal, timeline, and transparent pricing. Once agreed, our team manages the project from design and development through testing, deployment, and post-launch support.

Q4. How does AlienCode ensure data security and confidentiality?

Security and privacy are non-negotiable for us. We follow industry-standard security protocols, use encrypted data handling, and comply with international data protection regulations. Client data is never shared, and all information is handled with strict confidentiality.

Q5. Can AlienCode support my business after project delivery?

Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance, technical support, and updates to ensure your system continues to run smoothly and stays up to date with evolving technology and security standards. Our long-term partnerships are proof of our commitment to client success.

Q6. How much do your services cost?

Pricing depends on the project scope, technology stack, and specific business requirements. We always provide a detailed, upfront quotation with no hidden costs. Our goal is to deliver maximum value for your investment—without unnecessary extras.
